FAQ

Answers for parents & partners

A quick guide to sizing, shipping and wholesale onboarding. You can tweak these questions as your policies evolve.

What age group do you cover? +
We specialise in kidswear from age 1–10 years with size‑wise breakdowns (1–2Y, 2–3Y, 3–4Y, etc.) for a better fit across toddlers, juniors and tweens.
Are the fabrics kid‑safe and comfortable? +
Yes. We use soft, breathable cotton blends with kid‑safe trims and AZO‑free dyes so outfits feel gentle on sensitive skin while still looking vibrant after many washes.
How long does shipping take for retail orders? +
Retail orders are usually dispatched within 24–48 hours and delivered within 3–7 working days depending on your pincode. Express options can be enabled for metros.
How can I become a wholesale partner? +
Browse the wholesale section, add bulk packs to your cart and complete the enquiry flow. Our team will reach out with price slabs, MOQs and territory details within one business day.
Can I customise colors or prints for my store? +
For higher volume partners, we offer colourway tweaks and exclusive prints on select styles. Share your requirements in the wholesale enquiry and we’ll suggest feasible options.
